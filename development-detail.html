Property Description

Cavalli Tower by Damac Properties is the first of its kind development in Dubai Marina that presents 1, 2 and 3-bedroom beach and palm-facing apartments with premium amenities. This 70-story high-rise masterpiece is designed by the renowned Shaun Killa, an award-winning architect firm, and is made available for discerning few.
 
Enjoy the breathtaking views of Palm Jumeirah from the six exclusive design jet elevators that glide through the creative tower, giving you a vantage point of the stunning sea views. Soak up in the sun and take in the breeze, all just at your fingertips without leaving home. Enjoy access to the 900-square meter beach pool with pop-up F&B service and explore the perfect place to relax and rejuvenate yourself.

Lifestyle

Set within the renowned Al Sufouh, a high-profile neighbourhood in Jumeirah, Dubai. The locality is spread over 7 square kilometres towards the north-western side of Dubai Marina and offers hassle-free access to the rest of the city without facing any major problems.
 
Experience living in the only Cavalli-branded tower in the world that offers uninterrupted views of the Palm, Burj Al Arab and Dubai Skyline. Designed by the top of the mind, where you can experience the calm aura of an indoor waterfall and the accompanying water features all around this elegant space.
